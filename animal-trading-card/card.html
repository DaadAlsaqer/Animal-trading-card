Panda

Baby pandas are born pink and measure about 15cm. They are also born blind and only open their eyes six to eight weeks after birth.

  • Scientific Name: Ailuropoda melanoleuca
  • Status: Endanger
  • Population: Only 1000 Pandas exist in the wild and 60 in zoos
  • Food: Pandas feed mostly on bamboo, a tall woody plant full of fiber

Giant pandas have a distinctive black and white coat, with black fur around their eyes and on their ears, muzzle, legs and shoulders. Their thick, wooly coat helps to keep them warm in their cool mountain homes.
